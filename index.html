You'll learn how to make a cup instant noodle

You need: One cup of instant noodle, a boiling pot, and enough amount of water

Steps

    Get pocket money from your parents

    Buy one cup of instant noodle from K-Market - The best one is Shin Ramen

    Open the lid and pour the orange powder soup, which will make the noodle spicy

    Pour the ingredient soup with many genes

    Now you have to boil water: pour enough amount of water and boil it in the boiling pot

    When completely boiled, pour the water in the cup until the line on the cup

    Wait for three minutes; don't open the lid during that time!

    After three minutes, enjoy!
